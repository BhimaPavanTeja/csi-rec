CSI Design & IQ Competition

Event by Computer Society of India

Event Details

The CSI Design & IQ Competition is an exciting event for students to showcase their creativity and intellectual skills. The event is split into two parts: a design competition and an IQ test. Participants will have the opportunity to express their innovative ideas and compete for exciting prizes.
